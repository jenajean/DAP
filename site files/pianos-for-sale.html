WELCOME to our little corner of the fascinating world of pianos, piano service, and custom, player-centered instrument restoration.

We are a full-service piano business, offering complete restorative and maintenance service for pianos in the Los Angeles area, including----

  • tuning
  • voicing
  • repairs
  • action regulation
  • rebuilding
  • refinishing
  • piano rentals
  • moving & storage
  • accessories and humidity control
  • smoke and fire damage
  • insurance inspections and appraisal
  • PLUS
  • evaluation and custom remanufacture/restoration of vintage grand pianos
  • analysis and custom performance enhancement of grand piano actions---we are a licensed Stanwood Precision Touch Design shop
  • a performance and recording space for artists
  • AND
  • the sale of select new and used pianos

As you browse our site, and get a sense of who we are, you’ll see that we love to educate people about the beauty of complete piano service, custom piano restoration, and concierge piano sales. It’s important for us to let piano lovers know the truth about pianos as we see it, to clear up some of the myths, half-truths, and outright "untruths" that seem to pervade our business. We throw our doors open wide, so to speak, and allow our clients to hear and see everything we do.
Thank you for giving us the gifts of your time and attention---we appreciate it; if you like what we’re about, we’d love to speak with you. Until then, take care and enjoy your life.
